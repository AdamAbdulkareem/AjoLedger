How it works

From invite to payout in four steps

  1. 1

    Create or join a group

    Start a new savings circle or enter an AJO-XXXXXX invite code.

  2. 2

    Set up your payout bank

    Link the account where you receive your turn when the cycle pays out.

  3. 3

    Contribute on schedule

    Pay into your group's virtual account. Every payment is logged instantly.

  4. 4

    Receive your payout

    When it's your turn, funds are disbursed to your linked bank account.
